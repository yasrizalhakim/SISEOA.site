About SmartPlace

Launched in 2025 as a student entrepreneurship initiative, SmartPlace began with a vision to innovate in the field of home automation by designing smart solutions for modern living.

Our team is made up of passionate students specializing in IoT and automation, dedicated to developing user-friendly, efficient smart home systems.

We believe that smart technology should be within everyone's reach, so we focus on scalable and budget-friendly solutions that can grow alongside your lifestyle and needs.
